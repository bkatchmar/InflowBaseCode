How It Works

How InFlow works for you

InFlow is a milestone-based payment and secure workshare platform designed to help freelancers and clients have a better working relationship.

Create a legally binding contract

Our contracts make it easy to understand even the most challenging contract terms. Set terms for project scope, milestones, timeline, number of revisions, work ownership and more to keep yourself protected. Once your contract is built and complete, send to your client for E-Signature and keep a copy for your records.

Send to your client for E-Sign

Clients have the ability to read and E-Sign directly through our platform. Simplify the process and keep a signed copy for your records.

Work and Bill By the Milestone

Work towards your next project milestone Each time you reach a milestone send your work to client for review. Once the milestone has been accepted and the client pays the work will be automatically sent to them.

Simple payments

Get paid by credit card, debit card or ACH payment. Our payment platform runs on stripe so you can enjoy secure transactions, low fees and fast payouts.

Secure work preview

Our platform gives you the ability to preview watermarked work with clients without giving them access to source files. Clients can leave feedback and initiate payment without accessing your work providing protection against your work being used without payment or permission.

An All-In-One Transaction Platform

Manage payments, file transfers, work preview and contracts all through our easy to use solution. We make it easy to get paid in full faster by helping you improve how you work with clients.
